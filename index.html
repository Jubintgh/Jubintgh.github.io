My projects

Click to Checkout Coin Pal!

Click to Checkout the repo!

Coin Pal is inspired by PayPal based on the idea of a Peer-to-Peer blockchain economy. The main purpose of the app is to facilitate the transfer of crypto between the users without the need to include the wallet hash with each transaction (like Venmo). This is a concept app and no real Crypto is being used. The front end of this application was made with React and it follows Redux architecture. The backend was developed using Flask and PostgresSQL. The APIs are designed around REST principles. The final touch and design were also created using raw CSS.

Stack Oil flow is a social-based platform that allows users to utilize question and answer-based features to both share and gain Automotive based knowledge. The main goal of Stack Oil Flow is to cultivate an environment of automotive enthusiasts, both recreational and professional to come together over a shared passion.

Click to Checkout Spartner!

Click to Checkout the repo!

Spartner is inspired by OkCupid based on the idea of finding gym friends who you can train with! The main purpose of the app is to help gym-goers find friends for their sports activities based on a matching algorithm. Upon sign-up users are directed to fill out a questionnaire that will be used by the matching algorithm to assign a score for each user that is used by the algorithm
